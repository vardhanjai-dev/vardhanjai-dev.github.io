About

I'm a Senior Engineering Manager with 20+ years in the IT industry at Hewlett Packard Enterprise. I stepped into engineering management in 2020 and into a Senior Engineering Manager role in 2025 — building on years as a Technical Lead. Today I own three teams (RSDC, SADB and a dedicated AI Team), guiding 24+ engineers while balancing delivery, budget, and the growth of every person on the team.

My career spans Infosys, Capgemini and HPE — moving from hands-on .NET/SQL engineering into full-stack consulting, and then into leadership. That depth means I lead from understanding, not from a distance.

Today my focus is accelerating AI and automation to drive business growth and service margin — launching an AI team from scratch, modernizing applications to private cloud, and championing SAFe Agile ways of working. I believe in servant leadership, a growth mindset, and leading by example.

I recently completed an MBA in Business Analytics (BITS Pilani) and completed AI for Managers at IIM Bangalore — pairing engineering depth with business strategy.
